# Brand Overview

Zuzana Kalfus is an independent web designer focused on creating modern, structured and sustainable websites for entrepreneurs and service-based businesses.

The brand emphasizes clarity, simplicity and strategic thinking.

A website is treated as a business tool — not decoration.

---

# Brand Positioning

Independent web designer helping entrepreneurs build websites that are clear, functional and easy to manage.

Focus:

clarity  
structure  
strategy  
long-term usability

Avoid unnecessary complexity.

---

# Target Audience

Primary audience:

entrepreneurs  
consultants  
coaches  
service providers  
small business owners

They want:

a professional website  
clear structure  
simple maintenance  
a partner who understands business

They do not want:

technical chaos  
overcomplicated tools  
agency bureaucracy

---

# Core Promise

Web bez zbytečností.

# Key Messages
